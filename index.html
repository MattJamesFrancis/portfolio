Welcome

My name is Samuel Wolfe. I'm a multimedia production professional with a wide range of cinematography, directing, and web design experience.


As an electronic media & film student at Eastern Washington University, where I graduated with honors in 2015, I wrote, shot, and directed a number of short films. As a senior, I was granted the film department's "Best Achievement in Narrative" award, and three of my shorts went on to screen at over a dozen international film festivals, including one whose sole submission criteria was an emphasis on visual storytelling.


Throughout my time at college, I was also involved in a diverse range of freelance production work. I shot and edited content for a wide range of clients and events including weddings, live sporting events, nonprofit organizations, and concert venues.
