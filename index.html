Welcome

My name is Samuel Wolfe. I'm a multimedia production professional with a wide range of cinematography, directing, and web design experience.


As an electronic media & film student at Eastern Washington University, where I graduated with honors in 2015, I wrote, shot, and directed a number of short films. In my senior year, I was awarded the film department's "Best Achievement in Narrative" award, and three of my short films screened at over a dozen international film festivals, including one whose sole submission criteria was an emphasis on visual storytelling.


Throughout college, I was also involved in numerous feelance production endeavors. Today, I continue to build on that experience by shooting and editing for a wide range of clients and events including weddings, live sports, concert venues, and non-profit organizations.
